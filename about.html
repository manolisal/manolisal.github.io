About Us

This is a blog website about web development and anything else I find interesting and worth reading about computer science in general.

Let me introduce myself.

I am an undergraduate student in University of Piraeus living in Athens, Greece. The purpose of this website is NOT to make any profit by the ads as any other blog, but to implement and test technologies such as Google AMP, Service Workers, Node.js and various web optimization tools, for mobile web development and speed optimization in real life scenarios. This website is in fact my thesis on how a webpage can become as fast and robust as possible for mobile users and enchance their user experience as much possible, by a programmers point of view.

Why ads then?

The only reason this website has ads is to test their performance under the conditions mentioned above and enchance it.

What is this questionnaire?

The section Questionnare in the menu bar on the left is a list of questions created my me for the users and potential testers, in order to collect data about their experience on this website. If you want to contribute please be free to use this website as you wish and then fill the questionnaire.
